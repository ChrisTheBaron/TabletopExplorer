Tabletop Explorer

Welcome to Tabletop Explorer. A simple, game-agnostic tabletop map simulator. Optimised for Desktop and Tablets (mobile coming soon). Everything is stored in your browser, no internet connection needed, no servers to connect to, no accounts to create. Any changes will be saved in your browser, so everything will be here ready for your next session.

See the sections below for more information, or simply click the at the top to get straight into it. You can always get back here by clicking the at the bottom of the screen.

Zooming

Use the slider at the bottom of the screen to adjust the zoom level. There are also the following functions:

  • Fit zoom to screen height
  • Fit zoom to screen width
  • Fit zoom to screen size
