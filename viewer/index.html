Tabletop Explorer

Welcome to Tabletop Explorer. A simple, game-agnostic tabletop map simulator. Optimised for Desktop and Tablets (mobile coming soon). Everything is stored in your browser, no internet connection needed, no servers to connect to, no accounts to create. Any changes will be saved in your browser, so everything will be here ready for your next session.

See the sections below for more information, or simply click the at the top to get straight into it. You can always get back here by clicking the at the bottom of the screen.

Moving Tokens

Simply drag and drop the token anywhere on the map to move it around. You can also double tap the token to turn it 90°.

Adding Tokens

Click to bring up the 'Add Token' window. From here you can specify the label, size and number of tokens to add to the current scene. You can also specify a colour or an image to be used for the tokens.

Click to bring up the 'Add Favourite Tokens' window. From here you can quickly add any tokens that you marked as a favourite when you added it the first time. Use this for PC's and NPC's that might appear on multiple scenes.

Removing Tokens

Click 'Remove Tokens' to change modes. With this enabled, when you tap on a token it will be removed.

Zooming

Use the slider at the bottom of the screen to adjust the zoom level. There are also the following functions:

  • Fit zoom to screen height
  • Fit zoom to screen width
  • Fit zoom to screen size

Changing the Map

Click to select a new background image for the current scene.

Resizing the Map

Click and then select 'Change Background Size' to start the process to resize the background image.

  1. Input the size of the reference square
  2. Adjust the size of the square to match your background image
  3. Click to resize the background image to the correct size

Adding new Scenes

Click to add a new scene. You will need to give it a name and select a background image. Your current scene will be saved.

You can also organise your scenes into a simple folder structure.

Switching Scenes

Click and select the scene you wish to switch to. Your current scene will be saved.

Removing Scenes

Click and select the scene you wish to remove. You cannot remove the current scene, you need to switch to a different scene first.
